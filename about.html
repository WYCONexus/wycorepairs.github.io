About

A WYCO Story

WYCO Repairs exists to provide straightforward repair and maintenance help for homes and small property operations across the Des Moines Metro. The goal is simple: show up, assess the problem, and leave the space better than it was before.

The work focuses on practical fixes, small installs, and quality-of-life repairs that keep spaces functioning the way they should. Clear communication, realistic expectations, and solid workmanship are the foundation of every job.

WYCO Repairs is currently a one-person operation run by Sean Skipworth, bringing hands-on experience from both apartment maintenance and restaurant facility work.

Experience behind the work

Before launching WYCO Repairs, Sean spent 3+ years working in apartment maintenance, handling the day-to-day repairs and turnover work that keep multi-unit buildings running smoothly. That environment builds a strong ability to diagnose problems quickly, prioritize repairs, and keep things moving without unnecessary complexity.

In addition, 2+ years of restaurant maintenance experience added another layer of practical problem-solving. Restaurants demand quick thinking, durable fixes, and the ability to keep equipment and spaces functioning under constant use.

Together, those experiences shaped the approach behind WYCO Repairs: practical judgment, reliable work, and solutions that actually hold up.

The idea behind WYCO

WYCO began as a broader creative concept built around the idea of We Yield Creative Outcomes. While WYCO spans multiple interests and creative projects, WYCO Repairs is where that philosophy meets hands-on work.

Repair work is often treated as routine or purely mechanical, but good maintenance requires observation, creativity, and thoughtful problem solving. Every space, building, and repair situation is slightly different, and finding the right solution is part of the craft.

A small but important partner

WYCO itself was inspired by Sean’s dog, Wycott, whose name helped shape the WYCO identity. While Wycott isn’t out turning wrenches on job sites, he remains a constant reminder of the loyalty, consistency, and personality that the WYCO name represents.

Sometimes the best brands start with a good dog.

Who WYCO Repairs works with

  • Homeowners who need reliable help with repairs and maintenance tasks
  • Landlords who need consistent small-scale repair work
  • Property teams that value clear communication and clean execution

The focus stays on practical work done well, without unnecessary complication or inflated promises.
