    Service Scope & Work Agreement
    
              
    Please review the following agreement regarding the scope and limitations of our services. By selecting the checkbox below, you acknowledge and accept these terms.
    
            
    
          
    
        
    
      
    • 
      
  • 
        
    
          
    
            
    WYCO Repairs provides residential maintenance and installation services within clearly defined limits. Work will not begin without acknowledgment of these terms.
    
            
    General Service Boundaries
    
            
      
              
    • Work is limited to non-licensed residential maintenance tasks.
      • 
              
    • No structural modifications are performed.
      • 
              
    • No plumbing, electrical, or mechanical system alterations requiring licensing are performed.
      • 
              
    • If licensed work is discovered or required, service will pause until corrected by a licensed professional.
      • 
              
    • WYCO Repairs reserves the right to decline work deemed unsafe or outside scope.
      • 
              
    • All work must be accessible and safe within standard 6’ ladder reach.
      • 
              
    • No attic access, roof access, crawlspace entry, or concealed wall work is performed.
      • 
            
    
            
    Appliance Installation
    
            
      
              
    • New, out-of-box appliances only.
      • 
              
    • Installation limited to existing hookups.
      • 
              
    • No shutoff valve replacement.
      • 
              
    • No wall opening, cabinet drilling, or line rerouting.
      • 
              
    • No modification of internal appliance systems.
      • 
              
    • Water line connections limited to existing, accessible connections.
      • 
              
    • Licensed plumbing or electrical issues will pause installation until resolved.
      • 
            
    
            
    Lighting & Electrical
    
            
      
              
    • Installation on existing, secure junction boxes only.
      • 
              
    • Ceiling fans require existing fan-rated boxes.
      • 
              
    • No rewiring, connector splicing, or electrical modifications.
      • 
              
    • No attic wiring access.
      • 
              
    • Loose, damaged, or unsafe wiring will result in service refusal.
      • 
            
    
            
    Smoke / CO Detector Replacement
    
            
      
              
    • Replacement in same location only.
      • 
              
    • No rewiring or connector modification.
      • 
              
    • Client must supply compatible replacement units.
      • 
              
    • No electrical system upgrades performed.
      • 
            
    
            
    Caulking – Interior Wet Areas
    
            
      
              
    • Interior kitchen and bathroom wet areas only.
      • 
              
    • Surface removal and replacement of existing caulking.
      • 
              
    • Rear seam of toilet base intentionally left unsealed.
      • 
              
    • 24-hour cure time required before moisture exposure.
      • 
              
    • No structural mold remediation.
      • 
              
    • Surface-level mildew cleaning only.
      • 
            
    
            
    Drywall Repair
    
            
      
              
    • Maximum patch size approximately 1’ x 1’.
      • 
              
    • No full-sheet drywall installation.
      • 
              
    • Texture blending not guaranteed to perfectly match.
      • 
              
    • No structural drywall or framing repair.
      • 
              
    • Painting only if client supplies paint and requests application.
      • 
            
    
            
    Locks & Rekey
    
            
      
              
    • Lock replacement limited to existing cutouts.
      • 
              
    • No drilling new holes.
      • 
              
    • No structural door modification.
      • 
              
    • SmartKey rekey limited to compatible models.
      • 
              
    • Client-supplied hardware required.
      • 
            
    
            
    Liability & Risk Acknowledgment
    
            
      
              
    • WYCO Repairs is not responsible for pre-existing conditions, hidden damage, or outdated infrastructure.
      • 
              
    • If unsafe conditions are discovered, service will be paused or declined.
      • 
              
    • Client acknowledges older homes may contain fragile materials or outdated systems.
      • 
              
    • Minor cosmetic variations may occur in repair or installation work.
